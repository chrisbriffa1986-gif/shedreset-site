ShedReset, operated by Bull Enterprises (Malta). Registered office and company details available on request. Contact for all data protection matters: hello@shedreset.com.

ShedReset (operated by Bull Enterprises) is the “controller” of your personal data under Article 4(7) GDPR — we decide why and how your data is processed. We have not appointed a separate Data Protection Officer at this stage; the contact above handles all data requests.

4. What we do NOT do

  • We do not use your data for profiling or automated decision-making that produces legal or similarly significant effects on you (Art. 22 GDPR does not apply).
  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not load third-party advertising or analytics pixels (e.g. Meta Pixel, Google Ads tags) on the waitlist page. See Section 9.

5. How long we keep your data

We keep waitlist data for 24 months from the date you sign up, or until you unsubscribe — whichever happens first. After that, we delete or irreversibly anonymise it unless a longer retention period is required by law. This reflects the storage-limitation principle under Art. 5(1)(e) GDPR.

Page interaction/analytics data that is not tied to your identity (i.e. aggregate statistics) may be retained longer in anonymised form, since anonymised data falls outside GDPR’s scope (Recital 26 GDPR).

7. International data transfers

At this stage, we do not intentionally transfer your personal data outside the European Economic Area (EEA). However, if the email delivery provider we select is US-based, your email and engagement data may be transferred to and processed in the US. In that case, the transfer will rely on an adequacy mechanism recognised under Chapter V GDPR — typically Standard Contractual Clauses (Art. 46(2)(c) GDPR) or, where applicable, the EU-US Data Privacy Framework — and this notice will be updated to name the specific safeguard used before that processor goes live.

8. Your rights

Under Articles 15–22 GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access — get a copy of the data we hold about you (Art. 15)
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate data (Art. 16)
  • Erasure — ask us to delete your data (Art. 17)
  • Restriction — ask us to limit how we use your data (Art. 18)
  • Portability — receive your data in a portable format (Art. 20)
  • Object — object to processing based on legitimate interest, including analytics (Art. 21)
  • Withdraw consent — stop marketing emails at any time, free of charge (Art. 7(3))

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@shedreset.com. We will respond within one month (Art. 12(3) GDPR).

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with Malta’s supervisory authority: Office of the Information and Data Protection Commissioner (IDPC), Floor 2, Airways House, Triq Il-Kbira, Tas-Sliema SLM 1549, Malta. Email: idpc.info@idpc.org.mt · Phone: +356 2328 7100 · Web: idpc.org.mt. This is without prejudice to any other administrative or judicial remedy (Art. 77 GDPR).

9. Cookies and tracking

The Site uses first-party JavaScript only to record page interaction data (timestamps, scroll depth, section engagement, device type, browser, referrer). This code runs on our own domain, is not sold or shared with ad networks, and does not set third-party tracking cookies or pixels.

  • Strictly necessary technical storage (e.g. remembering that you already submitted the form, session integrity) is exempt from consent under ePrivacy Directive Art. 5(3), as implemented in Malta.
  • Any non-essential analytics storage that is not strictly necessary will only be activated with your prior consent, obtained via a separate cookie banner mechanism (to be implemented before launch if such tracking is added). At present, no such non-essential cookies are set.
  • We do not use third-party advertising pixels, retargeting tags, or cross-site tracking of any kind on this page.

10. Changes to this notice

We will update the effective date above whenever we make material changes to this notice, and — where required — seek fresh consent.
